üìä Executive Summary

Journeyman Jobs is a specialized mobile application designed exclusively for IBEW (International Brotherhood of Electrical Workers) union members. The app serves as a comprehensive job discovery platform with the mission of "Clearing the Books" - helping electrical workers find employment opportunities across North America quickly and efficiently.
