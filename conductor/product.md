# Product Guide - Journeyman Jobs

## Initial Concept
A simplified and centralized platform for IBEW journeymen to find work without having to navigate a complicated and archaic job referral system.

## Target Audience
- **IBEW Electrical Workers:** Journeymen, Linemen, Wiremen, Operators, and Tree Trimmers looking for stable or temporary work opportunities.
- **Storm Response Teams:** Workers specializing in emergency power restoration who need rapid updates on storm-related calls.

## Core Goals
- **Centralization:** Provide a single source of truth for job opportunities across different IBEW locals, eliminating the need to visit multiple disparate websites or call boards.
- **Simplification:** Replace archaic and manual referral processes with a modern, intuitive mobile interface.
- **Efficiency:** Reduce the time between a job posting and a qualified worker securing a referral.
- **Safety & Awareness:** Integrate real-time weather data and safety protocols to support workers in high-risk environments like storm restoration.

## Key Features
- **Real-time Job Board:** A comprehensive, searchable list of job postings with filters for classification, location, and job type.
- **Streamlined Referral Process:** A simplified workflow designed to modernize the referral procedure, allowing workers to secure referrals quickly without navigating complex legacy systems.
- **Storm Work Hub:** A dedicated section for emergency restoration work, featuring priority listings and integrated NOAA weather radar/alerts.
- **Union Directory:** A complete, searchable directory of IBEW locals with integrated contact and location information.
- **Push Notifications:** Instant alerts for new job postings matching user preferences and critical storm work opportunities.
